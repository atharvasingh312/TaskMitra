Summify
Summify helps users to get a summary of large texts. Summify is designed to save users time by providing a condensed version of lengthy articles. By using advanced algorithms, Summify analyzes the content and identifies the most important information, which is then displayed in a concise format.
SocioBlock
SocioBlock can be used to block access to social media websites such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and more. By blocking these distractions, Socioblock helps users to stay focused and productive while working on their tasks.
